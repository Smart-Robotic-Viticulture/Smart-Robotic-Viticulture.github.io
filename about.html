What do we do

We aim to improve the update of technology in viticulture in a sustainable manner by developing new algorithms, tools, techniques and systems which will free up farmer time for doing what they love best - growing grapes and making good wine. We also aim to increase our understanding of the factors which govern yield, maturity and disease so that these challenges can be better tackled by the industry.
