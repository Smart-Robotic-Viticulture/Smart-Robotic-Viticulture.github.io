Successful Applications

    Pernod Ricard, the world's second largest winemaker, used data we mined from historical yield estimates to refine their data collection practices in the Marlborough region of New Zealand.

    SwarmFarm applied algorithms we developed for apple flower and canopy density mapping as part of the variable rate sprayer project which is being tested on a commercial orchard in Shepparton, Victoria.

    Plant and Food Research New Zealand used QR code based image processing tools we developed to dramatically simplify data collection in large scale trials for leaf and trunk disease.

    Algorithms we developed for stomate detection and analysis are being used by researchers in paleoecology in the Netherlands.

    Results from field testing of Satellite Based Augmentation Systems were used to inform the Australian Government decision to expand SBAS support in the region.

    Komatsu Japan is implementing patented earth moving algorithms we co-developed with Jose Guivant and Jayantha Katupitiya.
