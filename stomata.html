Automated Stomata Analysis

 

Stomatal density and behavior are good indicators of the overall health of a plant as well as its surrounding environment. Microscope images are often used to analyze stomatal behavior in plants. However, most of the current approaches involve manual measurement of stomatal features. The main aim of this project is to develop fully automated stomata detection and pore measurement techniques using microscope images of plant leaf surface as the input.

Related Publications

Jayakody H;Liu S;Whitty M;Petrie P, 2017, 'Microscope image based fully automated stomata detection and pore measurement method for grapevines', Plant Methods, vol. 13.
